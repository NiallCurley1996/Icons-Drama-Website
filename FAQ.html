FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions


Q.1 What is the earliest age that Icons Drama can take in students?

The earliest age is six years, but if your child will be turning six during the term they can apply.


Q.2 How long is each class?

The length of each class varies from day-day-day.

If you want to know more about class times, visit the "Classes" page found in the navbar at the top of the website.


Q.3 Where can I make a booking?

Visit the bookings page to make a booking for an available class. We have classes every day Monday-Friday.


Q.4 What sort of clothes do children need?

Children can wear there own clothes to any of our classes. We will provide
a free Icons Drama t-shirt to any new kids who have joined one of our classes.


Q.5 How can I contact Icons Drama?

Check out our 'Contact Us' page in the top navigation bar, all our contact details are there. You can make
use of our unique "ask a question" feature if you want to send us an email.
