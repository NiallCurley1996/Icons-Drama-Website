Child Protection Information

The safety of your child is paramount to us at Icons. All our staff and volunteers have had all necessary Access NI checks carried out and have completed the 'Keeping Safe' child protection course before working with your children.Icons has a designated person who handles all child protection issues and any concerns are acted upon immediately.
