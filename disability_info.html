Disability Friendly


Everyone Welcome!!

At Icons Drama we enjoy working with children of all abilities. Our belief is that every child is an important part of our community and we work with them to bring out their hidden potential.

Autism Awareness

Icons Drama is an organisation that is 'Autism Aware'. We seek to integrate all our children on the spectrum at every level. Recent studies have shown that drama helps children within the austic spectrum develop their communication and interpersonal skills.

Mencap

We at Icons are in partnership with Mencap and other disability organisations to bring together children that have disabilites with those that don't. Our family ethos creates a sense of openness which all our children thrive in. In the classes we take all our young people develop together by breaking down ignorance and myths about disabilities. All our staff have carried out training through the Volunteer Development Agency and are sensitive to the needs of children with disabilites.
